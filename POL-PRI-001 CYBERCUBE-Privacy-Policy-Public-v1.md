## 5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information in
the following circumstances:

### 5.1 Service Providers

We share information with vendors who help us operate our business:

| Provider Type           | Purpose                |
| ----------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Cloud hosting providers | Store and process data |
| Payment processors      | Process payments       |
| Email service providers | Send communications    |
| Analytics providers     | Analyze usage          |
| Customer support tools  | Provide support        |

All service providers are bound by contracts requiring them to protect your
data and use it only for specified purposes.

### 5.2 Business Transfers

If CYBERCUBE is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your
information may be transferred. We will notify you of any change in
ownership or use of your personal data.

### 5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or if we believe disclosure is
necessary to:

- Comply with legal process or government requests
- Enforce our agreements and policies
- Protect the rights, property, or safety of CYBERCUBE, our users, or others
- Detect, prevent, or address fraud or security issues

### 5.4 Advertising and Analytics Partners

We may share certain information (such as device identifiers, cookie data,
and usage information) with advertising and analytics partners to deliver
relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness. This may
constitute "sharing" under the California Consumer Privacy Act. You can opt
out of this sharing through our cookie preferences, by using the Global
Privacy Control signal, or by clicking the "Do Not Share My Personal
Information" link on our website.

### 5.5 With Your Consent

We may share your information for other purposes if you give us explicit
consent.

## 9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 9.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.
We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience and
analyze usage.

### 9.2 Types of Cookies We Use

| Category                     | Purpose                               | Examples                                 |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| **Strictly Necessary** | Essential for the website to function | Authentication, security, load balancing |
| **Functional**         | Remember your preferences             | Language, theme, saved settings          |
| **Analytics**          | Understand how you use our services   | Page views, feature usage, performance   |
| **Marketing**          | Deliver relevant advertisements       | Ad targeting, campaign measurement       |

### 9.3 Your Cookie Choices

**Browser Settings:** You can control cookies through your browser settings.
Blocking all cookies may affect website functionality.

**Cookie Banner:** When you visit our website, you can manage your cookie
preferences through our cookie consent banner.

**Opt-Out Links:**

- Google Analytics: [https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- Industry opt-out: [https://optout.aboutads.info](https://optout.aboutads.info)

### 9.4 Do Not Track and Global Privacy Control

Our website does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals.
However, we honor the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal as a valid
opt-out of the sharing of personal information for targeted advertising
under applicable law, including the California Consumer Privacy Act. You
can also control tracking through cookie preferences and browser settings.
